PRISM Partner

Moroccan-compliant partner management for PRISM ERP

Overview

The PRISM Partner module extends the standard partner model with Moroccan-specific fields and functionality. It provides a comprehensive set of features to manage customers and vendors in compliance with Moroccan regulations.

Partner Type Selection

Easily categorize your contacts:

  • Customer
  • Vendor
  • Both Customer and Vendor

Each type has its own dedicated view and menu for easy access.

Moroccan Legal Information

Store and manage essential legal identifiers required for Moroccan businesses:

  • ICE Number (Tax ID)
  • RC Number (Trade Register)
  • CNSS Number (Social Security)

With built-in validation to ensure data integrity.

Enhanced Contact Information

Maintain detailed contact information:

  • Contact Person
  • Job Title
  • Department
  • Bank Information

Payment Management

Configure payment and credit settings:

  • Payment Terms
  • Credit Limit
  • Partner Category (A/B/C)

Invoice Tracking

Monitor outstanding invoices:

  • Due Invoices Count and Amount
  • Overdue Invoices Count and Amount
  • Quick Access to Invoice Lists

Multiple Views

Access partners through different views:

  • Kanban View with Photo and Key Information
  • List View with Customizable Columns
  • Detailed Form View

Key Features

  • Comprehensive partner profile with Moroccan-specific fields
  • Separate menus for customers and vendors
  • Due and overdue invoice tracking
  • Enhanced search capabilities
  • Partner categorization
  • ICE number validation
