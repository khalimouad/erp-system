PRISM Company

Moroccan-compliant company management for PRISM ERP

Overview

The PRISM Company module extends the standard company model with Moroccan-specific fields and functionality. It provides a comprehensive set of features to manage company information in compliance with Moroccan regulations.

Legal Information

Store and manage essential legal identifiers required for Moroccan businesses:

  • ICE Number (Tax ID)
  • RC Number (Trade Register)
  • CNSS Number (Social Security)
  • Legal Form (SARL, SA, etc.)
  • Regional Classification

Business Classification

Categorize your company based on Moroccan business classifications:

  • SME Status
  • Exporter Status
  • Free Zone Location

Tax Management

Configure tax-related settings specific to Morocco:

  • Tax Exemption Status
  • Exemption Type and Expiry
  • VAT Filing Frequency

Accounting Settings

Configure accounting preferences for Moroccan operations:

  • Accounting Method
  • Fiscal Year Start Date

Social Media

Manage your company's online presence:

  • Website
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • LinkedIn

Key Features

  • Comprehensive company profile with Moroccan-specific fields
  • Integrated with other PRISM modules for consistent data usage
  • User-friendly interface for managing company information
  • Support for multiple companies with different configurations
