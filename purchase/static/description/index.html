PRISM Purchase

Moroccan-compliant purchase management for PRISM ERP

Overview

The PRISM Purchase module extends the standard purchase functionality with Moroccan-specific fields and features. It provides a comprehensive set of tools to manage purchase orders in compliance with Moroccan regulations.

Order Type Classification

Categorize purchase orders based on their type:

  • Standard Orders
  • Import Orders
  • Free Zone Orders
  • Government Orders

Each type has specific behavior and tax implications.

VAT Reverse Charge

Handle VAT reverse charge according to Moroccan tax law:

  • Import Purchases
  • Free Zone Purchases
  • Non-resident Vendor Purchases

With automatic tax calculation and reporting.

Import Management

Comprehensive import handling:

  • Customs Declaration Tracking
  • Customs Value and Duty
  • Import VAT
  • Customs Codes
  • Country of Origin

Delivery and Shipping

Manage international trade terms:

  • Incoterms (EXW, FOB, CIF, etc.)
  • Shipping Methods
  • Origin Countries
  • Expected Delivery Dates

Approval Workflow

Implement approval processes for:

  • High-Value Orders (> 100,000 MAD)
  • Import Orders
  • Free Zone Orders
  • Discount Validation

With tracking of approvers and approval dates.

Multi-Currency Support

Handle multiple currencies with MAD conversion:

  • Exchange Rate Management
  • Automatic MAD Conversion
  • Dual Currency Display

Key Features

  • Comprehensive purchase order management with Moroccan-specific fields
  • VAT reverse charge handling according to Moroccan tax law
  • Import and customs management
  • Approval workflow for high-value and import orders
  • Multi-currency support with MAD conversion
  • Enhanced reporting capabilities
  • Integration with other PRISM modules
