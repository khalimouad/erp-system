PRISM Inventory

Moroccan-Compliant Inventory Management

PRISM Inventory extends Odoo's inventory management capabilities with features specifically designed for the Moroccan market.

Key Features

Warehouse Management

  • Warehouse type classification (standard, bonded, free zone, transit)
  • Customs information tracking
  • Temperature and humidity control
  • Capacity monitoring
  • Inspection scheduling

Location Management

  • Location type classification (standard, customs, quarantine, temperature, hazardous, valuable)
  • Access control for restricted locations
  • Temperature and humidity monitoring
  • Capacity tracking
  • Inventory scheduling

Lot/Serial Tracking

  • Origin tracking (domestic, imported, free zone)
  • Customs document tracking
  • Expiry date management
  • Quality control workflow
  • Quarantine management

Transfer Management

  • Customs-controlled transfers
  • Quality check requirements
  • Approval workflows
  • Temperature-controlled transportation
  • VAT exemption handling

Inventory Operations

  • Inventory type classification
  • Approval workflows for sensitive operations
  • Scheduling and planning
  • Multi-currency valuation (MAD support)
  • Enhanced reporting

Moroccan Compliance

PRISM Inventory is designed to meet Moroccan regulatory requirements for inventory management, including:

Customs Regulations

Compliant with Moroccan customs requirements for bonded warehouses, free zones, and transit operations.

VAT Compliance

Support for VAT exemptions and special VAT handling for different warehouse types and operations.

Traceability

Enhanced traceability for regulatory compliance, with origin tracking, lot management, and approval workflows.
