PRISM Accounting

Moroccan-compliant accounting module for PRISM ERP

Overview

PRISM Accounting is a comprehensive accounting module that is fully compliant with Moroccan accounting standards. It provides all the necessary features to manage your company's finances.

Key Features

  • Moroccan Chart of Accounts
  • VAT management
  • Corporate Tax (IS) management
  • Income Tax (IR) management
  • Multi-company support
  • Multi-currency support
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Asset management
  • Budget management
  • Analytic accounting
  • Financial reporting
  • Tax reporting
